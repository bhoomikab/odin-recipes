Garlic Udon Noodles

Description

Garlic Udon Noodles are a quick, savory dish packed with bold garlic flavor and chewy, satisfying udon noodles. Tossed with a simple soy-based sauce and garnished with green onions, this recipe is a delicious go-to for a comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • 14 oz udon noodles (fresh or frozen)
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • Sesame seeds for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the udon noodles according to package instructions, drain, and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, melt butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.
  3. Stir in soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sugar. Mix well.
  4. Add the cooked udon noodles to the pan and toss until fully coated in the sauce.
  5. Garnish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds if desired.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
