Garlic Dinner Rolls

Description

Garlic rolls are soft, buttery, and packed with savory garlic flavor, making them a perfect side for any meal. Made with simple ingredients like flour, yeast, butter, garlic, and parsley, they’re easy to whip up but incredibly satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp butter, melted
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let sit for 5 minutes until frothy.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and knead until a soft dough forms.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rise for 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Divide the dough into small rolls and place on a baking sheet.
  5. Mix melted butter with minced garlic and parsley. Brush the rolls with this mixture.
  6. Bake for 15-18 minutes until golden brown.
  7. Serve warm and enjoy!
