Garlic Bread

Description

Caprese Garlic Bread is a fresh and flavorful twist on classic garlic bread, topped with juicy tomatoes, creamy mozzarella, and fragrant basil. It’s the perfect appetizer or side dish, combining the essence of a Caprese salad with the comfort of garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of French bread, halved
  • 3 tbsp butter, melted
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2-3 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Mix melted butter and minced garlic, then spread evenly over the halved bread.
  3. Toast the bread in the oven for 5-7 minutes, or until golden and crisp.
  4. Top with slices of fresh mozzarella and tomato. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Return to the oven for 5-8 minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and drizzle with balsamic glaze if desired.
  7. Slice and serve warm!
